First you must know what branding and to-brand means. Branding is the promotion of a particular product or company by means of advertising and distinctive design. The process involved in creating a unique name and image for a business in the consumers' mind, mainly through advertising campaigns with a consistent theme. Branding aims to establish a significant and differentiated presence in the market that attracts and retains loyal customers.

Branding from day one is very important!

You are your brand. What people say when you leave the room is your brand. Making yourself visible is very important when it comes to branding. The primary purpose of branding is to create loyalty. Loyalty means repeat purchases and loyal fans who will advertise your brand for free... (Ex: when a client checks-in or shares your business page)... Branding is more than just a logo, it is important and paves the way visually, it is emotional, it is how a guest feels about the service that they receive. How the guest sees the style. How the guest ties in the smell of a product with your branding.

How the guest feels = Branding

Tips to help build your brand:

  1. Be Consistent
  2. Incorporate your brand in everything you do
  3. Produce Value
  4. Build your online platform
  5. Remain relevant

Be smart with brand-building awareness:

  • Consistent branding - ensure that your branding elements (logo, colors, fonts and tone) are consistent across all platforms. This creates a cohesive identity that will be easily recognizable.
  • Content Marketing: Create valuable and relevant content that resonates with your target audience. This could be blogs, videos, infographics or podcasts that establish your brand as an authority in your industry.
  • Social Media Engagement: Utilize platforms like Facebook, Instagram, Twitter, and LinkedIn to engage with your audience. Regularly post updates, respond to comments and share user-generated content to foster a community around your brand.
  • Influencer Marketing: Collaborate with influencers who align with your brand values. Their endorsements can introduce your brand to a broader audience and lend credibility.
  • Search Engine Optimization (SEO): Optimize your website and content for search engines to improve visibility. Good SEO practices ensure that your brand appears in relevant search results.
  • Email Marketing: Build an email list and send regular newsletters or promotional content.
  • Public Relations: Share press releases and newsworthy stories about your brand to gain media coverage.
  • Community Involvement: Participate in local events or sponsor community initiatives.
  • Consistent Branding: Ensure that your branding elements are consistent across all platforms.
  • Paid Advertising: Invest in paid ads on social media or search engines to reach specific demographics.
  • Partnerships and Collaborations: Team up with other brands or organizations that complement your offerings.
